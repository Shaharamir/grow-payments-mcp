# Overview

Recurring payments allow businesses to automate ongoing charges, such as monthly fees, subscriptions, or other regular customer payments.

It is needed when a business wants to automate monthly billing, reduce manual work, and issue an automatic invoice for every charge.

The flow starts by creating the payment form with a unique pageCode, then presenting the returned payment link in an iframe, new tab, or direct customer message.

Businesses can edit the recurring payment details at any time, keeping recurring billing flexible and easy to manage.
Uniquely with Grow, customers can also set up the recurring payment through PayBox, without entering credit card details.

Effortless Billing with Grow Systems  
Experience the simplicity of billing with Grow Systems' streamlined direct debit system.  
Creating and managing direct debits is a breeze, allowing you to customize the frequency according to your needs.

**Key Features:**  
● Create Direct Debits:  
○ Initiate a new direct debit effortlessly.  
○ Specify the desired frequency for the direct debit to occur.  
● Edit Direct Debit:  
○ Easily modify or cancel the existing direct debit through the Edit Direct Debit feature.

### Important Information:

● This option is specifically designed for recurring payments, such as monthly fees.  
● When setting up and configuring billing, include the monthly fee in the sum parameter.  
● Server-to-server confirmation is generated only during the initial billing setup.  
● For monthly confirmations, set up a Webhook and provide us with the address. Refer here for more details.
