# Static Payment Page

> Tailor-made Sales and Payment Pages for Effortless Transactions

At Grow, we empower businesses with dynamic solutions tailored to their unique needs. Whether it's a product, service, or event, our Static Payment Pages offer a seamless way to set up sales and payment pages swiftly.

**Varieties of Our Static Sales/Payment Pages:**  
● Image and Text Sales Page:

- Present your product or service with captivating images and informative text.

● Text-Only Abbreviated Page:

- A clean, text-based sales page for a quick and straightforward presentation.

● Photo Billing Page:

- Focus on visual appeal by showcasing photos without intricate product details.

● Payment Collection Page:

- Streamline the payment process with a dedicated collection page.

---

## **Customization Options:**

Tailor your sales pages with precision, defining key parameters to enhance user experience:

- Product Imagery: Showcase your product or service through compelling visuals.
- Business Logo: Display your brand identity prominently.
- Product/Page Name: Clearly communicate the identity of the offering.
- Product Description: Craft engaging and informative text about the product or service.
- Pricing Structure: Specify product pricing based on quantity.
- Purchase Limits: Control the quantity of products customers can buy at once.
- Stock Information: Set a closing time for the page based on stock availability.
- Discounts: Implement quantity or date-specific discounts.
- Payment Options: Enable multiple payments or recurring payment facilities.

Embed your sales page seamlessly on external websites using Redirect (not Iframe) for a cohesive user experience.  
Grow brings sophistication to your sales and payment processes, ensuring a user-friendly and efficient transaction experience.
